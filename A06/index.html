Palawan

The Philippines' Last Ecological Frontier

Top Destinations

El Nido

Explore stunning limestone cliffs, hidden lagoons, and crystal-clear waters. A paradise for island hopping.

Coron

Famous for its world-class wreck diving sites, pristine lakes like Kayangan, and breathtaking landscapes.

Puerto Princesa

Home to the world-renowned Underground River, this city blends natural wonders and adventure perfectly.

Tubbataha Reefs

A UNESCO World Heritage site and a diver's dream, boasting unparalleled marine biodiversity and vibrant coral reefs.

Port Barton

A tranquil and laid-back village offering a peaceful escape with its untouched beaches and charming simplicity.

Balabac Islands

The southernmost tip of Palawan, offering exotic, unspoiled islands with Maldives-like sandbars and rich wildlife.
