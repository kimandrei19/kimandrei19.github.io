Boracay Island

Discover Paradise in the Philippines

The Gem of the Visayas

Famous for its powdery white sand beaches, crystal-clear turquoise waters, and vibrant nightlife, Boracay is a world-renowned tropical paradise that promises an unforgettable escape.

Must-See Spots

White Beach

The island's 4-kilometer main beach, known for its stunning beauty and vibrant atmosphere.

Puka Shell Beach

A quieter northern beach famous for its namesake puka shells and serene ambiance.

Mount Luho

Hike to the highest point on the island for breathtaking 360-degree panoramic views.

Bulabog Beach

Asia's premier destination for kiteboarding and windsurfing, with strong, consistent winds.

Crystal Cove Island

A nearby private island perfect for cove exploration, trekking, and snorkeling adventures.

Diniwid Beach

A charming and secluded cove just a short walk from the bustling White Beach.

Things to Do

Island Hopping

Explore nearby islands, snorkel in clear waters, and enjoy a fresh seafood lunch.

Sunset Watching

Witness one of the world's most beautiful sunsets from a paraw sailboat or a beachside bar.

Food Tripping

Savor everything from fresh seafood grills on the beach to international fine dining cuisines.
