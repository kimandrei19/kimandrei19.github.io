Boracay Island

Paradise in the Philippines

White Beach

The island’s most famous beach, known for its powdery white sand, clear waters, and vibrant mix of nightlife, water activities, and sunsets.

Puka Shell Beach

A more peaceful and natural escape, featuring coarse sand with puka shells, fewer crowds, and a laid-back vibe perfect for relaxation.

Mount Luho

Boracay’s highest viewpoint, offering panoramic views of the island’s beaches and forests—ideal for sunrise/sunset watching and photo ops.

Bulabog Beach

Located on the island’s eastern side, this is Boracay’s adventure sports hub, famous for kiteboarding and windsurfing, especially during the Amihan season..

Crystal Cove Island

A small island getaway with crystal-clear waters, hidden coves, and caves to explore—perfect for day trips, snorkeling, and photo spots.

Diniwid Beach

A quiet, charming beach just north of White Beach, offering tranquil waters, cozy resorts, and a more private, romantic atmosphere.
